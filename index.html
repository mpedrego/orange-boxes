I was born and raised in Phoenix, AZ. My entire extended family has been in the landscaping and construction business for over 40 years. My very first job was digging holes, and I have probably dug over 100 holes!

My passion: I love music, art and love encouraging people to express their passions. I host a podcast called, "The Outlet" where we interview hard working local artists and give them a platform to share their stories.

While growing up in the urban part of Phoenix, I noticed the community lacked rescoures for the youth. My goal is to eventually create a rec center for Maryvale, to give them the opprotunities to create art with an outlet to express themselves in a safe environment!
